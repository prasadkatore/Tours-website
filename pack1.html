MANALI

4 Nights,5 days
Chandigargh to chandigarh

Departure dates:-

May 2018-25,28

Country:-India
Cities & Landmarks :-Chandigargh,Manali,Rohtang pass,solang valley,etc.
Day 1:-Chandigargh Airport / Station - Hotel  

Arrive at Chandigargh Airport or Thivim Station and proceed for your transfer to your hotel. Then we proceed to Manali by enjoying a scenic drive through Pandoh Dam and Kullu Valley. En route we visit Kullu Shawl Factory where you can buy woollen products. Late night check in Manali Hotel.

Day 2:-Manali – mall road  

Today we explore the ancient temples like Vashista Kund and Hadimba Mandir. We have free time for shopping in the evening on mall road.
Day 3:-Manali – Solang Valley – Manali  

Today we proceed to Solang Valley – A splendid valley between Solang village which offers the view of glaciers and snow-capped mountains. We enjoy Ropeway ride at Solang Valley. Later enjoy the optional adventure activities like Horse riding, Skiing, Ice skating, Paragliding, mountain bike, snow scooter, Zorbing at your own. Free time to explore Manali.
Day 4:-Manali - Rohtang Snow Point – Manali  

Today we enjoy spectacular drive towards Rohtang snow point. Free time to play and enjoy in the snow. Please Note: Rohtang pass is 51 kms. Away from Manali. This Pass is not accessible for almost 8 months in a year as it is covered by snow. We generally visit the snow point on Rohtang road, which has been permitted by the Border Security Force. Entertain yourself through spectacular Himachali folk dance.
Day 5:-Manali - Chandigarh (1 Night)  

Today we proceed to Chandigarh - the capital of Punjab and Haryana We enjoy water rafting in Beas river (subject to operation). Drop on airport/station.Tour concludes.!!!
